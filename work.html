What do the 5 circles in the olympics logo represent?
The five interlocking rings in the Olympic logo represent the five continents of the world (Africa, the Americas, Asia, Europe, and Oceania) coming together in unity and solidarity through the celebration of the Olympic Games. The colors of the rings (blue, yellow, black, green, and red) were chosen because every country's flag contains at least one of these colors.
